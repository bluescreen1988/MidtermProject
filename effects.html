Casulties

The Thirty Years War is considered one of the worst medical failures in European history.The war killed civilians and soldiers alike through bothe battle, plague, and famine killing around eight million souls in total. These events exacerbated the social tensions that had been forming throughout Europe.

Political Consequences

The Peace of Westphalia brought the war to an end in 1648. The war had lasting effects on the power structure within Europe. The states within the Empire became even more decentralized after the end enjoying greater freedoms from Imperial rule. The territory of nations also became more clearly structure and defined.
